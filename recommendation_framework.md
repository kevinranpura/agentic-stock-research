# Stock Recommendation Framework

BUY recommendation:
- Market trend should be bullish.
- Positive news should support the move.
- Volume should confirm price action.
- Prefer stocks with strong catalysts.

Avoid BUY:
- Price increased sharply without reason.
- Weak trading volume.
- Negative sentiment exists.

HOLD recommendation:
- Mixed signals between market and news.
- Long-term outlook positive but short-term uncertain.

SELL recommendation:
- Bearish trend.
- Negative sentiment.
- Heavy selling pressure.

Confidence scoring:

80-100:
Strong agreement between price action, volume, and news.

60-80:
Mostly positive signals but some risks.

Below 60:
Unclear direction or conflicting indicators.
