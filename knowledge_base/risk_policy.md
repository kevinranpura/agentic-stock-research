# Stock Risk Policy

Every recommendation must include risks.

Technology sector risks:
- Reduced client spending.
- Currency fluctuations.
- AI disruption.

Banking risks:
- Rising NPAs.
- Interest rate changes.
- Regulatory changes.

Manufacturing risks:
- Raw material price increase.
- Demand slowdown.

Avoid:
- Guaranteed return claims.
- Risk free recommendations.
- Overconfidence without supporting data.
